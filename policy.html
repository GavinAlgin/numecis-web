Privacy Policy

At Numecis, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to safeguarding the information you share when using our mobile application and website. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. About Numecis

Numecis helps users understand the behavior of numbers by patternising, interrelating, interlinking, and precising numerical data. Lottery numbers carry significant information — they come and disappear, interact, appear in groups or as singles, much like friends. Our platform empowers users to make wiser, safer, and more fruitful decisions when betting or playing lottery number games.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account details (name, email, username)
  • Device information and logs
  • Usage interactions within the app
  • Optional user-provided numerical or game-related data

3. How We Use Your Information

Data collected is used to:

  • Enhance app accuracy and performance
  • Provide personalized number analysis
  • Deliver notifications and important updates
  • Strengthen user safety and system security

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. While no digital system is fully immune to breaches, we continuously work to prevent unauthorized access or misuse.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your information. Some data may be shared with trusted service providers for analytics, hosting, or performance enhancements under strict confidentiality requirements.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and application may use cookies or similar technologies to monitor usage patterns and improve your experience. You may disable cookies through your browser settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

Numecis does not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 18. If such data is found, we will remove it promptly.

8. Third-Party Links

Our platform may include links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their content, privacy practices, or policies. We recommend reviewing their privacy statements before using their services.

9. Your Rights
